Understanding What You Feel

Anxiety & Tension

Anxiety can manifest as racing thoughts, a rapid heartbeat, or persistent worry. Understanding these physical and mental signals is the first step toward finding balance.

Feeling Overwhelmed

When low moods or emotional weight make daily life feel sluggish, learning gentle grounding habits can help bring back a sense of peaceful energy.

Daily Practice Tools

4-7-8 Deep Breathing

Inhale quietly for 4 seconds, hold your breath for 7 seconds, and exhale completely for 8 seconds. This biological hack instantly triggers your body's relaxation system.

Guided Journaling

Writing down worries moves them from internal loop logic to structured external paper. Spend 5 minutes daily answering: "What is one simple thing that brought me ease today?"

Crisis & General Support

If you or someone you love is in physical danger, call emergency services immediately. For urgent mental health support in the UK, call Samaritans on 116 123 or text SHOUT to 85258.
